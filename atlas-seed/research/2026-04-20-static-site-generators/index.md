> **Decision:** Astro for content-heavy marketing sites; Hugo for large doc portals; Next.js for hybrid static/dynamic apps.

## The short list

| Generator | Language | Signature strength |
|---|---|---|
| Astro | JS/TS | Zero-JS HTML with selective hydration |
| Hugo | Go | Sub-second builds for thousands of pages |
| Next.js | JS/TS (React) | Hybrid SSG + SSR + ISR |
