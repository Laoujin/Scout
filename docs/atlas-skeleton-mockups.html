Pairing notes. Skeletons and card variants are orthogonal — any card works inside any skeleton. The combinations I'd actually consider: S1+V1 (Atlas today, polished), S2+V1+V7-toggle (high-throughput), S3+V7 (docs-like, filter-heavy), S5+V3-featured+V1-below (editorial), S6+V4 (full terminal identity).

Palette notes. "Warm rust" stays closest to the current logo's temperature. "Paper & ink" leans archival (good if cover art is mostly muted/desaturated). "Cartography" is the most on-brand for "Atlas" but narrows mood. "Midnight" is the only dark option — commit fully or skip. "Minimal" is the neutral doc-site pick. "Field notes" reads as "scout's lab notebook" — specific but charming.
